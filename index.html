FAQ

Will you remove 100% of my information?

No service can guarantee 100% removal. Some sites lack opt-out paths or quickly re-list data. Our goal is to remove as much as possible and keep monitoring new appearances.

How long does removal take?

Many sites process within days; others take weeks. We send updates as statuses change.

Do you cover social networks?

We focus on data brokers and people-search sites. We provide guidance for securing your social profiles.

What do you need from me?

Just your basic identity details to verify removals (name, city, state). We minimize what we store and delete it upon request.
