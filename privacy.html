Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-26

ClearTrace (“we,” “us”) provides personal data removal and monitoring services. We take privacy seriously and only collect information necessary to provide our services.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide (name, email, location) for identity matching and support.
  • Operational data (sites we scanned, removals requested, confirmations).
  • Basic analytics (aggregate, non‑identifying) to improve our site.

How we use information

  • To perform scans, submit opt‑out requests, and monitor re‑listings.
  • To communicate progress and provide reports.
  • To comply with legal obligations (CCPA/CPRA, GDPR where applicable).

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not share information with third parties except to process opt‑outs or as required by law.

Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact us at support@clear-trace.net.

Data security

We follow industry‑standard safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.
